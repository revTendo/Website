We are happy to announce that we are releasing our proxyless patch!

Download revNet.zip

So here's how to connect:

  1. Prepare your SD card
    Drag the 3ds and cias folders to the root of your SD card. The folders are inside the combined_out folder.
  2. Handle file conflicts
    If your file manager asks to replace existing files when dragging the 3ds folder, click replace.
  3. Install revNet (FBI)
    Open FBI on your 3DS, navigate to the cias folder on your SD card, and install the revNet CIA. It will appear on your HOME Menu.
  4. Alternative: Homebrew Launcher
    If you prefer not having revNet on your home screen, open the Homebrew Launcher and launch the revNet app from there.
  5. First launch & update
    Open revNet. It should display a message that it has updated. Press Start to reboot your console.
  6. Switch to revTendo
    After the reboot, open the revNet application again and switch the active service to revTendo.
    For users coming from Brewtendo: Press Y to unlink your BNID before switching. You cannot use both services at the same time.
  7. Enjoy!
    You are now connected to revTendo. Have fun!
    If revNet errors out after switching, simply reboot your console again.

If you experience any issues, please report them in the #support channel on our Discord.

You need to be in the Discord server for security reasons and support.
