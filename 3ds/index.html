How to connect to reBadge?

Requirements:
• a working Nintendo 3/2DS (emulators are not supported)
• a working internet connection
• credentials from https://pine-cone.xyz/reBadge

How to connect:
Turn on your 3DS, go into the internet settings, select your connection, and set a proxy. IP is 89.167.6.41, Port is 8081. Then, click on the right arrow on the touch screen, enable Basic Authentication, and set the username and password that the website gave you. Once done, open Badge Arcade and you're in!
