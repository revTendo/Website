Support

Stuck connecting, or something broke? Start here.

Get help
  • Read the FAQ first. Most connection problems are answered there.
  • Check the setup steps for your console: 3DS or Wii U.
  • Still stuck? Ask in the Discord. Say which console you have and what you have already tried.
Report something broken

Open a ticket in the Discord and include:

  • Your console model and system version.
  • What you were doing when it broke.
  • Any error code the console showed you.

Never share your proxy password with anyone, including staff.
