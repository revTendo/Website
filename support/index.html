Support

- Stuck? -
  • Check the FAQ first, most connection problems are in there.
  • Go over the setup steps again: 3DS or Wii U.
  • Still nothing? Ask in the Discord, and say which console you have and what you already tried.
- Something broke -

Open a ticket in the Discord and tell us:

  • your console model and system version
  • what you were doing when it broke
  • any error code the console showed you

Never give your proxy password to anyone, staff included.
