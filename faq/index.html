- FAQ -
What is revTendo?

A revival of the online services Nintendo shut down for the 3DS and Wii U. We host the servers the consoles used to talk to, so the eShop and Badge Arcade work again.

Does it work on emulators?

No, emulators are not supported.

Why do I have to be in the Discord to get credentials?

The proxy got stolen once already. Leaving it out in the wild is a nice way for that to happen again. This will change with the release of the Inkay and 3DS patches.

When can I use it without a proxy?

With the Inkay and 3DS patches. Progress is tracked on the 3DS and Wii U progress pages.

Can I use it on my Wii U right now?

Not yet. The eShop runs on real hardware, but there is nothing to install. The steps go on the Wii U page once they are ready.

Something is not working.

Head over to Support.

Who runs this?

The staff, all volunteers.
