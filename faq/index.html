FAQ

The questions we get asked most.

- Questions -
What is revTendo?

A fan-run replacement for Nintendo's discontinued 3DS and Wii U online services. We host the servers the consoles used to talk to, so the eShop and Badge Arcade work again.

Does it work on emulators?

No. The 3DS setup needs real hardware.

Why do I need to be in the Discord to get credentials?

The proxy has been stolen before. Handing out open access is how that happens again. Credential generation stays behind the Discord until the Inkay and 3DS patches ship.

When can I use it without a proxy?

Once the Inkay and 3DS patches are released. Progress is tracked on the 3DS and Wii U progress pages.

Can I use revTendo on my Wii U today?

Not yet. The eShop runs on real hardware in testing, but there is nothing to install. Setup instructions go on the Wii U page when they are ready.

Something isn't working. What now?

Head to Support.

Who runs this?

The staff, all volunteers.
